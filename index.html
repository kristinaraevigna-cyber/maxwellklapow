Digital behaviour change, built for real-world systems.

Maxwell C. Klapow — Oxford researcher working on mental health interventions at scale.

I design, scale, and evaluate digital interventions that deliver mental health and behaviour change support inside the systems people already use. My doctoral work at Oxford tests how evidence-based mental health content can be embedded within a WhatsApp-based parenting programme running across the Mexican public health system, in partnership with UNICEF and the Mexican federal government.

I also run an independent consulting practice advising health tech companies, NGOs, foundations, and governments. If you are building something in this space, start on the Consulting page.
